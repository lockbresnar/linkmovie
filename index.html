    How to Play

    1) Two actors from the target movie are shown at the top.
    2) You have 6 tries to guess the correct movie.
    3) Each guess or hint uses a try.
    4) Correct guess = green, win popup.
    5) Out of tries = lose popup with correct answer.
    6) Daily mode = same movie and actors for everyone that day.
    7) Infinite mode = new random movie each time.

    About/Contact | Privacy Policy

    Link.Movie is a simple, fast, and endlessly replayable game built around the idea of connecting actors through the movies they have appeared in. Each round presents two actors, and your challenge is to trace a chain of film connections that links them together using cast members they have worked with. Some pairs are straightforward because they have appeared in well-known ensemble films, while others require deeper thought or broader knowledge of cinema. The game encourages you to recall not only major roles, but also supporting actors who often bridge different genres and eras.

    Every puzzle is an opportunity to explore the hidden structure of the movie world. Many actors appear in unexpected roles, and discovering the paths that lead from one actor to another can reveal relationships between films that you may never have noticed before. Link.Movie appeals to movie fans, trivia lovers, and anyone who enjoys a problem-solving challenge that rewards creativity and curiosity.

    The game is approachable for casual players but also rewarding for those who enjoy improving their speed or finding the shortest possible connection between two actors. Whether you're drawing on your own memory or learning something new along the way, Link.Movie offers a flexible experience that suits both quick play sessions and longer, more thoughtful attempts.

    With continuously refreshed actor combinations and unlimited replay value, the game provides a fun and engaging way to discover how interconnected the movie world truly is.

    How to Play Link Actor

    Link.Actor is one of the game modes available on the Link.Movie website. This page provides an interactive puzzle where users connect two actors by entering movies that link their shared cast appearances. The game is based on real film credits and is designed as a simple, browser-based tool for users who are interested in film data and movie-related puzzles.

    To begin, you are given a starting actor and a target actor. Your goal is to find a path between them by entering movies that connect their shared cast members. Start by entering the name of a movie that the starting actor appeared in. From that movieâ€™s cast, the game determines the next valid actor in the chain.

    Continue the process by entering movies that feature the current actor. Each movie you enter advances the chain by linking actors who appeared together in that film. You repeat this process until you reach the target actor. The objective is to complete the connection using as few steps as possible, with each valid movie counting as one step in the chain.

    Your final score is calculated using both the number of steps taken and the time required to complete the puzzle. Solutions that use fewer steps and less time result in better scores. You can replay the puzzle to try different paths, improve efficiency, or explore alternative connections between actors using different films.

What is Movie.Link?

Movie.Link is a set of quick film trivia games you can play in your browser.

Movie Link

Find the correct movie. You’ll see stats like tries, time, and points. Daily mode supports sharing.

Actor Chain

Connect the start actor to the target actor through movies that share cast members. Your score is based on steps and time. Daily mode supports sharing.

Multiplayer

Optional timed rounds with a lobby, countdown, and scoreboard.

Credits

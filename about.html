What is LinkMovie?

LinkMovie is a pair of quick film games you can play in your browser, with optional multiplayer.

Link Movie

2 actors are connected by the same movie. Guess the correct movie.

Link Actor

Connect the start actor to the target actor through movies that share cast members. Your score is based on steps and time.

Multiplayer

Play short timed rounds with friends.

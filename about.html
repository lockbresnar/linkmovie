What is LinkMovie?

LinkMovie is a pair of quick film games you can play in your browser, with optional multiplayer.

Movie Link

Guess the correct movie. You’ll see stats like tries, time, and points. Daily mode supports sharing.

Actor Chain

Connect the start actor to the target actor through movies that share cast members. Your score is based on steps and time. Daily mode supports sharing.

Multiplayer

Play short timed rounds with friends: lobby → countdown → play → scoreboard.

What is Movie.Link?

Movie.Link is a set of quick film trivia games for browsers. We currently have two core games, plus an optional multiplayer mode.

Movie Link

Find the correct movie. You’ll see stats like tries, time, and points. In Daily mode you can share your result.

Actor Chain

Connect the start actor to the target actor through movies that share cast members. Your score is based on steps and time. In Daily mode you can share your result.

Multiplayer (optional)

Play short rounds with friends. A lobby, a countdown, timed play, and a scoreboard decide the winner.
